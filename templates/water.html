💡 Hydration Tips

🌅 Morning: Start with 500ml water before breakfast — it kickstarts metabolism and reduces hunger.
🍽️ Before meals: 500ml of water 30 min before eating can reduce calorie intake and aid digestion.
🏋️ Exercise: Drink 150-250ml every 15-20 min during workouts to maintain performance.
☀️ Hot days: Add an extra 500ml on days above 35°C or when you sweat more than usual.
🫖 Counts: Chai, buttermilk, dal, and fruits all count toward your daily fluid intake.
🚫 Signs of dehydration: Dark urine, fatigue, headaches, and difficulty concentrating.
